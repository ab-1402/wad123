About the Department

The Department of Information Technology at AVCOE is committed to producing competent IT professionals. Our curriculum emphasizes both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing students to thrive in industry and research. The department encourages innovation, critical thinking, and entrepreneurship.

Objectives

  • Provide quality education in Information Technology
  • Promote research and development
  • Encourage student participation in technical and cultural events
  • Establish strong industry-institute collaboration
